ABOUT ME

Yinka Ash is multifaceted, a designer, artist, creative director and Founder of Ashcorp Luxury Projects Limited, the holding company for Ashluxe, Vertical Rave and Ashluxury. As a key opinion leader within his industry, he paves the way for young audacious creators who dare to leave a timeless mark on the global tapestry.

EARLY LIFE & EDUCATION

Ash was born on January 7, 1995.
Biochemistry – Caleb University, Lagos – 2014
Luxury Brand Management – University of Arts, London – 2020.

CAREER

Yinka Ash blazes the trail of young entrepreneurs in Africa’s pret-a-porter market. His interest in design and fashion crystallized during creative during community service during summer school in Germany circa 2006. Ash’s affinity towards style, enthusiasm for perfection & top quality constitutes Ashluxe’s quintessence, which is characterized by details, tailoring, graphics, design and quality.

ASHLUXE

ASHLUXE is a luxury fashion brand, a community, but more so an art project. As an art project, ashluxe is not confined to being one thing or unidirectional. As long as we stay true to our ethos and our core identity, our offerings and products can be expressed via various mediums but right now the primary medium is fashion. This means as we evolve over time, our offerings will diversify beyond clothing into art, product design, and music.

YAART PROJECT

Yinka Ash Art Research and Technology project (YAART) is a continuous stream of Ash’s creative consciousness and an expression of his thoughts through tangible but diverging projects without the constraints of commercialization or obeisance to convention, trends or analytical projections based on data.

It is his freedom, his most honest unfiltered voice, a middle finger to the constricting laws of business. Ash’s test lab. A place where no rules are sacrosanct except his commitment to free his mind of every idea through his hands. The tenets for this space remain the values I hold true; community, innovation and excellence.

YAART INCUBATION PROGRAMME

The Yinka Ash ART (YAART) Incubation Program is a nonprofit organization, established in 2021, working to inspire and empower youths and individuals, to hone the skills of young minds in a move to educate & mentor the next generation of African creatives. With over 1000 registered Mentees across Africa, Led by Ash and the senior management team at Ashcorp.
